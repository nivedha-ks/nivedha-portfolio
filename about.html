Thesis Highlights

Designed advanced photonic crystal fiber (PCF) and photonic quasicrystal fiber (PQF) based sensors using the Finite Element Method. Achieved high sensitivities in both infrared and terahertz regions. Developed steering-wheel microstructured PCF sensors with a relative sensitivity up to 95%, demonstrating strong potential for chemical and biological sensing.
